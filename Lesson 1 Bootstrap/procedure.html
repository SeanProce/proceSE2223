Procedure


Step 1

 To operate the device, the bottle that the user wishes to open should be placed on the table directly under the circular hole at the bottom of the prototype.

Step 2

 The two sliding pillars should then be squeezed from the top or bottom until it is no longer locked in the holes of the box. The sliding piece should then be slid up or down until the cap of the bottle falls inside the ring at the bottom.

Step 3

 Once the correct pair of holes is located, release both pillars so that the piece gets locked at the height of the holes.

Step 4

 Then the rod should be screwed in using the handle until the bottle's cap is tightly between the end of the rod and the wall of the ring. Once the cap is tightly secured, the user can turn the body of the bottle until the cap comes loose.
